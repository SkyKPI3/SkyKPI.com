Privacy Policy

How SkyKPI handles information you share through this website. Last updated June 2026.

The short version

We collect contact information you give us through our contact form. We use it to reply to you. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than working with you. There are no tracking cookies, no ad pixels, and no analytics tools that follow you around the internet.

What we collect

When you fill out our contact form, we collect the name, company or association name, email address, area of interest, and message you provide. That is it.

When you visit our website, our hosting provider Netlify logs basic technical information including IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This is standard server log activity used to keep the site running and secure.

What we do with it

We use the contact form information to respond to your inquiry, schedule a conversation if appropriate, and follow up about the work we might do together. We keep the information in our email system and customer records as long as we have a business relationship or active conversation.

What we do not do

We do not sell your information to anyone.

We do not share your information with third party advertisers or marketers.

We do not use tracking pixels, third party advertising cookies, or behavioral tracking tools.

We do not subscribe you to a mailing list without your direct consent.

Service providers

We use a small number of trusted service providers to run our business. These include Netlify for website hosting and form handling, and a standard business email provider for replying to you. These providers have their own privacy policies and are bound by their own contracts to keep your information secure.

Cookies

This website does not set tracking cookies. The site may set basic functional cookies required for the form to work or for the site to display properly. No advertising or analytics cookies are used.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete the information you have shared with us at any time. Email hello@skykpi.com with the request and we will remove your records within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

This website is for business use and is not directed at children under thirteen. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle information, we will update this page and update the date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@skykpi.com.
