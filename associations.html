Association Management

Associations outgrew the one person model.

Members expect more. Events got more complex. Communications need to be constant. One executive director cannot carry all of it well. With SkyKPI your association gets a full team across operations, membership, events, finance, and communications for one monthly fee.

The Reality

One person, eight jobs, a stretched budget.

Most association directors carry the workload of a small company. Member onboarding, event logistics, sponsorship calls, newsletters, board prep, financial coordination, vendor management, and strategy. When one person owns all of it, the strategic work gets pushed to the side and the day to day eats the calendar. An AMC fixes that by spreading the work across specialists.

Association FAQ

What boards ask before they hire us.

How does AMC pricing work?

Most engagements are a monthly retainer scoped to the association size, event load, and service mix. Some functions like major event production bill on top of the retainer. We provide a clean scope and a clear number so the board knows exactly what they are paying for.

What happens to our current staff?

It depends on the situation. Some associations transition fully to the AMC model. Others keep an executive director and use the AMC as a force multiplier across functions the director cannot cover. We handle the conversation with care.

Do you handle our finances?

We coordinate financial operations including AP, AR, budget tracking, and board reporting. Tax preparation and audit work stay with your accountant or auditor. We make their job easier with clean books and timely data.

How do you handle our existing AMS or website?

We work with what you have unless it is genuinely broken. When a system change is the right move, we manage the evaluation, selection, and implementation so the board does not have to learn AMS shopping from scratch.

Can we keep our identity and culture?

Yes. The association is yours. We run the engine. The board still sets direction. Members still see your name, your brand, your voice. We are the operations behind the scenes.

Is your board having the AMC conversation?

If your director is stretched, your events are getting harder to run, or your strategic plan keeps slipping, talk to us. We will tell you straight if an AMC makes sense.
