About SkyKPI

A firm built for one industry. On purpose.

Most agencies and association management firms sell to anyone with a checkbook. We do not. SkyKPI exists because the AEC industry, especially precast concrete, deserves a firm that already speaks the language and already knows how decisions get made.

Construction is a relationship industry that runs on credibility, depth, and follow through. The buyers are smart. The decisions are technical. The dollars are real. A marketing partner who has never set foot in a precast plant or read a CSI spec is going to spend the first six months of a retainer learning what an insider already knows.

SkyKPI started because that gap costs producers and associations real money. The owner of a precast plant should not have to explain what a double tee is. An association director should not have to brief a vendor on how AIA continuing education works. The job of a partner is to already know the basics so the conversation can move to strategy.

What we focus on

Our primary focus is marketing for precast concrete producers and AEC suppliers. That is the deepest specialty we have. We build the websites, run the social, produce the content, administer the continuing education, and manage the spec stage positioning that gets a producer in front of architects and engineers months before the RFQ.

Our secondary focus is association management for construction industry trade groups. Many of the same skills apply. Member communication, event production, sponsorship sales, financial coordination. We bring those capabilities to associations that have outgrown the single executive director model.

How we work

SkyKPI is boutique on purpose. Senior people run every account. There are no juniors learning on your dime. The team is small enough that the people who pitched you are the people doing the work. Retainers are scoped clearly. Travel and major content production days bill separately and transparently. There are no surprise charges.

We answer email. We show up on time. We do the work. We tell you the truth when something is not working. That is the standard. The industry has had enough of agencies that disappear after the contract signs.

Where we are

SkyKPI is headquartered in Frankfort, Illinois, thirty minutes south of Chicago. The Midwest precast and AEC corridor runs from the Twin Cities through Chicago down to Indianapolis and Cincinnati. We are central to all of it. We travel for clients across the United States and have the camera and drone equipment to produce real content on site instead of relying on stock.

Who we work with

Our clients are confidential. We do not publish a client list because the work we do, especially competitive market positioning and spec stage strategy, is the kind of thing producers prefer their competitors not see. If you want references during a serious conversation, we will provide them.

What we will not do

We will not take work outside the AEC industry. We will not run a junior account team. We will not promise viral content or vanity metrics. We will not pretend a campaign worked when it did not. The specialty and the standards are how the firm earns its keep.

Want to talk?

The fastest way to find out if SkyKPI is the right fit is a direct conversation. No deck. No discovery call theater. Just a real discussion about what you are working on.
